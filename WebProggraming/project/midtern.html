Agents

The numbers in the "No." column are in the chronological order of when the agents were recruited by the VALORANT Protocol. You can find their numbers in game by going to their contracts and looking at their banners. There is an eighth agent in the VALORANT Protocol but their identity is unknown, and they are not yet a playable agent in VALORANT.

Five agents are immediately available for players to use; Brimstone, Jett, Phoenix, Sage, and Sova. All other agents must be unlocked by progressing through each of their agent contracts and completing Tier 5.
